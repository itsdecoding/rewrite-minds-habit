Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2025  ·  Effective immediately

LifeFlow is built with your privacy in mind. We collect only what's necessary to run the app, and we never sell your data to anyone.

Information We Collect

When you use LifeFlow, we may collect the following:

  • Account information — your name and email address when you sign up or sign in with Google.
  • App data — habits, tasks, journal entries, and goals you create within LifeFlow.
  • Usage data — basic analytics such as feature usage and session duration to help us improve the app.
  • Device information — browser type, operating system, and device type for compatibility purposes.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve LifeFlow's features and functionality.
  • Sync your data securely across devices.
  • Send important service-related communications (no spam).
  • Respond to your support requests.

Google Sign-In

LifeFlow offers sign-in via Google OAuth. When you use this option, we receive your name and email address from Google. We do not receive your Google password, and we do not access any other Google account data beyond what you explicitly authorize.

Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, a trusted cloud infrastructure provider. We use industry-standard encryption (HTTPS/TLS) for all data in transit. Your app data is stored on servers located in secure data centers.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share data only with:

  • Service providers — such as Supabase (database) and Vercel (hosting), who process data on our behalf under strict confidentiality agreements.
  • Legal requirements — if required by law or to protect the rights and safety of our users.

Cookies & Local Storage

LifeFlow uses browser local storage to save your preferences (such as theme settings) and to maintain your authentication session. We do not use third-party advertising cookies.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data.
  • Export your app data at any time (via the export feature in Settings).
  • Withdraw consent at any time by deleting your account.

Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, your personal data will be removed within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.

Children's Privacy

LifeFlow is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We'll notify you of significant changes by posting the new policy on this page with an updated date. Continued use of LifeFlow after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
