Refund Policy

Last updated: May 2026  ·  Simple, honest, and risk-free

❤️ We want you to absolutely love using LifeFlow OS! We're committed to delivering a beautiful, distraction-free environment to elevate your daily productivity. If you upgrade to Pro and feel the experience isn't the right fit for you, we want to make it right without any stress or hard feelings.

1. The 14-Day Risk-Free Window

We offer a 14-day, 100% money-back guarantee on all of our premium plans (LifeFlow Pro Monthly, Yearly, and our Lifetime Pass). If you cancel your subscription or contact us within 14 days of your initial purchase date, we will process a full refund—no tricky questions asked, and no high-pressure sales pitches.

2. Payment & Billing Partners

All payments are securely handled by Lemon Squeezy, our globally verified, trusted merchant of record. This ensures your credit card, Apple Pay, and purchase data are protected by bank-level, encrypted payment gateways. When a refund is approved by our team, Lemon Squeezy handles the transfer back to your original payment method automatically.

3. How to Request Your Refund

We believe getting a refund should be just as easy as signing up. There are no complicated forms to fill out. Simply:

  • Send a quick, friendly email to itsdecoding@gmail.com.
  • Include the email address connected to your LifeFlow OS account or your Lemon Squeezy order number.
  • Once received, our customer support team will process your refund within 24 to 48 hours. It may take 3 to 7 business days to appear on your bank statement depending on your card issuer.

4. Easy Subscription Management & Cancellation

If you're loving the app but just want to turn off auto-renewal for your subscription (Monthly or Yearly), you can do it instantly with one click:

  • Open your Settings Panel inside the LifeFlow OS dashboard.
  • Click Manage Subscription to access your secure Lemon Squeezy billing portal.
  • Click Cancel Subscription.

You will retain complete access to all Pro features until the end of your current billing period, and no future charges will occur.

5. Fair Play & Exceptional Cases

This policy is designed to give new users absolute peace of mind. We reserve the right to decline refund requests in extremely rare cases of clear system abuse (such as subscribing, exporting all habits/data, requesting a refund, and repeating the cycle multiple times). We keep this policy open and fair to protect our independent development team.

6. Got Feedback? We'd Love to Hear It!

If you choose to refund because something was confusing or you missed a specific habit tracking feature, please tell us! We improve LifeFlow OS based directly on user reviews, and your ideas could help shape the future of the app.
